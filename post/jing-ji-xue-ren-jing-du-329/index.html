经济学人精度 3.29

2022-03-30
                                                             Unit-1 
                                                   🌈Day2 知识点梳理

【1】 impose sth on sb 强加
impose silence on the class
强制让班里静下来
impose a heavy burden on their lives
给生活带来沉重负担
impose quarantine for sb.
对某人进行隔离

【2】 crushing
suffer a crushing defeat
遭遇惨败
The restriction rules were a crushing blow to malls. 遭遇沉重打击
be in crushing debt 债台高筑

【3】 mark 是...的迹象、成为...的征兆、标志着
Today marks the 80th Anniversary of the Social Security Act.
今天是《社会保险法案》的80周年纪念日。

【4】 splinter = divide 使分裂

【5】 potent 强大的

【6】 trigger

【7】 prompt 引发、激起（=trigger）；促使

【8】unprecedented 前所未有的
The instant immiseration of a big economy is unprecedented and will cause alarm around the world.
让一个大型经济体一夜之间赤贫化是前所未有的，这将给世界敲响警钟。

【9】 priority
Ensuring that our people have enough food remains a top priority for our government.
解决好吃饭问题始终是头等大事。
In our work this year, we must make economic stability our top priority and pursue progress while ensuring stability.
今年工作要坚持稳字当头、稳中求进。

【10】 doctrine 信条;主义;学说;尤指宗教教义
Then it must create a doctrine to govern these weapons in order to prevent a broader shift towards autarky.


🌈Day3 知识点梳理

【11】 take...seriously; take...for granted
The fact that Russia did not take the threat of sanctions seriously at first is no surprise.
俄罗斯一开始并没有将制裁威胁看在眼里，这并不令人意外。

【12】 plentiful
Migrants to cities are attracted by plentiful jobs, access to hospitals and education, and the ability to escape the boredom of a farmer's agricultural life.
进城务工的民工被大量的就业机会、医疗和教育资源，以及逃离枯燥的农业生活所吸引。

【13】 be subject to 受...支配；易受...影响
Some 10,000 people or firms are subject to American sanctions, affecting over 50 countries with 27% of world GDP, and covering everything from torture to cryptocurrencies. 约有一万个人或企业受到美国制裁，影响到50多个国家，这些国家的GDP占全球的27%，涵盖了从刑讯到加密货币等方方面面。

【14】 torture
Some 10,000 people or firms are subject to American sanctions, affecting over 50 countries with 27% of world GDP, and covering everything from torture to cryptocurrencies.
约有一万个人或企业受到美国制裁，影响到50多个国家，这些国家的GDP占全球的27%，涵盖了从刑讯到加密货币等方方面面。

【15】 evade
Autocrats can evade targeted measures. 独裁者可以避开那些有针对性的措施。

【16】 crippling
Full embargoes on Iran and Venezuela have been crippling but not toppled regimes.
对伊朗和委内瑞拉的全面禁运虽然削弱了政权实力，却并没有彻底推翻。

【17】 topple 推翻；倒下
Once he nearly toppled down a flight of stairs.（原版书《了不起的盖茨比》）
有一次他差点从楼梯上滚了下去。

【18】 关于pressure的几种搭配
给...施加压力 apply/put/place pressure on sb.
躲避压力 escape, get away with, evade


🌈Day3 知识点梳理

【19】 cross the Rubicon 越过雷池、破釜沉舟、断然采取行动
On February 26th that Rubicon was crossed, when sanctions were imposed on the world’s 11th-biggest economy.
就在2月26日，美国越过雷池，对世界第11大经济体进行制裁。

【20】 expel 开除、取消比赛资格、驱逐出境、排遣
Cultivating positive interpersonal relationships helped him expel solitary feelings.
培养积极的人际关系帮助他摆脱孤独感。

【21】 plumbing 管道系统
By making it illegal for Western firms to deal with big Russian banks, except in the energy trade, and expelling them from the global-payments plumbing, the flow of money across borders is seizing up.
禁止西方企业与俄罗斯大型银行进行交易（能源贸易除外），把俄罗斯从全球支付系统中踢出，这些措施导致跨境资金流动停滞。

【22】 seize up
By making it illegal for Western firms to deal with big Russian banks, except in the energy trade, and expelling them from the global-payments plumbing, the flow of money across borders is seizing up.
禁止西方企业与俄罗斯大型银行进行交易（能源贸易除外），把俄罗斯从全球支付系统中踢出，这些措施导致跨境资金流动停滞。

【23】 access 核心是 opportunity, right
男人和女人应该享有平等的受教育和就业的权利：No man or woman should be denied equal access to education and employment.

【24】 reserve
未知全貌，不予置评：Reserve all judgements until you see the whole picture.

【25】 evaporate
His courage / hopes has(have) evaporated.
他丧失了勇气/希望。

【26】 threaten
The fighting is threatening to turn into full-scale war.
这场战斗有可能会演变为全面的战争。

【27】 offshore
There have been signs of stress in offshore dollar-funding markets, meaning foreign central banks may ask the Federal Reserve for dollars.
有迹象显示，离岸美元融资市场面临压力，这意味着外国央行可能会向美联储索要美元。

【28】 关于“公司”的说法
company, firm 公司
giant, corporation, behemoth 大公司
group, conglomerate 集团
