                                                Unit-1 Day2 知识点梳理

【1】 impose sth on sb 强加
impose silence on the class
强制让班里静下来
impose a heavy burden on their lives
给生活带来沉重负担
impose quarantine for sb.
对某人进行隔离

【2】 crushing
suffer a crushing defeat
遭遇惨败
The restriction rules were a crushing blow to malls. 遭遇沉重打击
be in crushing debt 债台高筑

【3】 mark 是...的迹象、成为...的征兆、标志着
Today marks the 80th Anniversary of the Social Security Act.
今天是《社会保险法案》的80周年纪念日。

【4】 splinter = divide 使分裂

【5】 potent 强大的

【6】 trigger

【7】 prompt 引发、激起（=trigger）；促使

【8】unprecedented 前所未有的
The instant immiseration of a big economy is unprecedented and will cause alarm around the world.
让一个大型经济体一夜之间赤贫化是前所未有的，这将给世界敲响警钟。

【9】 priority
Ensuring that our people have enough food remains a top priority for our government.
解决好吃饭问题始终是头等大事。
In our work this year, we must make economic stability our top priority and pursue progress while ensuring stability.
今年工作要坚持稳字当头、稳中求进。

【10】 doctrine 信条;主义;学说;尤指宗教教义
Then it must create a doctrine to govern these weapons in order to prevent a broader shift towards autarky.
